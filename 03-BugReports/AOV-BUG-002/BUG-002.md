## Description

During Peak Tournament matchmaking, the player is automatically placed into the match even if the **Ready** button is never pressed.

The Ready Check confirmation is expected to require explicit player interaction before entering Hero Selection.

---

## Preconditions

- Peak Tournament mode is available.
- Player account is eligible to enter Peak Tournament.
- Internet connection is stable.

---

## Reproduction Steps

1. Open Arena of Valor.
2. Enter **Peak Tournament** mode.
3. Start matchmaking.
4. Wait until the **Ready** confirmation appears.
5. Do **not** press the **Ready** button.
6. Wait until the countdown expires.

---

## Expected Result

If the player does not confirm **Ready** before the countdown ends:

- The player should not enter the match.
- The matchmaking should be cancelled or handled according to the intended game design.
- Appropriate penalties (if applicable) should be applied.

---

## Actual Result

The player is automatically transferred into the match even though the **Ready** button was never pressed.

---

## Impact

This issue bypasses the Ready Confirmation process.

Possible impacts include:

- Players unintentionally entering matches.
- AFK incidents caused by unintended match entry.
- Negative competitive experience.
- Reduced reliability of the matchmaking confirmation system.
